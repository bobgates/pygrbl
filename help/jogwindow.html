On the jog tab, there are 12 buttons: which are up and down for each of 3 axes, at each of two step sizes. Three step sizes are available and are configurable on the preferences page. The buttons with a single arrowhead select the middle step size. The double arrowhead selects the large step size. The small step size is only available via the keyboard.

Each of the buttons has a shortcut key on the cursor pad and another on the numeric keypad. The x-axis buttons are mapped to left and right, and to 4 and 6 on the numeric keypad. The y-axis buttons are mapped to up and down, and to 8 and 2 on the numeric keypad. The z-axis buttons are mapped to page-up and page-down and to plus and minus on the numeric keypad.

Each of the keys on the keyboard moves the corresponding axis by the middle step size when used on its own. If shift is pressed simultaneously, the large step size is used. To obtain the small size step, the buttons are pressed while pressing the apple or command key.

It is often not clear whether the jog controls are active. In active mode, the controls appear in a frame, as in the figure above, and the word 'Active' appears in the bottom right corner.
